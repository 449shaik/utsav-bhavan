VENUE

Our newly renovated venue has all of the bells and whistles to make every event one of a kind and special. With years of industry experience, we have targeted all of the pain points for clients and addressed them with our new technology and setup. Special features including a bridal suite for relaxing after hours of photoshoots and dancing and a speaker system which projects sound directly onto the dancefloor making it easier for guests in the seating area to have conversations. At the end of the day, our venue is best experienced in person and our friendly staff is always happy to show you around.

Menu

Utsav boasts a diverse menu not found in any other hall. Prior to the acquisition and renovation of the hall, we went around and explored menus from many venues and restaurants. When we finally gathered our list of dishes, we set out on a mission to source the best ingredients and find the master chefs who would be able to prepare them. Our menu ranges from Mediterranean to Asian cuisine, which is always made to order using the freshest ingredients.
