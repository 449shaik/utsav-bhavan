THE CREATER

Chief Creative Director

The visionary behind Utsav Bhawan began planning destination weddings in 2009, at a time when the concept was still emerging in Dhanbad. With a natural flair for artistic balance and a deep understanding of the luxury event segment, the Founder has consistently led the planning team to craft breathtaking celebrations with unmatched precision and execution. Driven by a mission to bring innovation to the event management industry, the goal has always been to deliver exceptional services at the most competitive prices. Every idea and element curated by the team is custom-tailored and unique, ensuring that every event is a one-of-a-kind experience. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for creating unforgettable moments, the Founder has orchestrated some of the most memorable weddings and social events—both across India and internationally.
